Our Story  ·  ストーリー

A Passion for
Authentic Flavors

Milan Restaurant was founded with a single vision: to bring the true, uncompromised taste of Indian and Nepalese cooking to the people of Saitama. Our founder, a native of Nepal with deep roots in Indian culinary tradition, arrived carrying nothing but a handful of treasured spice recipes and an unwavering passion for authentic cooking.

Over fifteen years, we have grown from a small neighbourhood eatery into a beloved dining destination, welcoming thousands of guests each month. Yet our philosophy remains unchanged: every dish must taste exactly as it would in the streets of Kathmandu or the kitchens of Rajasthan.

We blend this culinary heritage with the Japanese value of omotenashi (おもてなし) — the art of anticipating a guest's needs before they even ask. At Milan, you are not just a customer; you are an honoured guest.

Our Values  ·  価値観

What We Stand For

🥬

Fresh Every Day

All curry bases and sauces are made fresh daily. We never use frozen or pre-packaged ingredients — quality is our non-negotiable standard.

Our Kitchen  ·  キッチン

Crafted with
Love & Tradition

At the heart of Milan Restaurant is our traditional clay tandoor oven — the soul of Indian cooking. Fired to temperatures exceeding 480°C, it produces the characteristic char and smokiness that makes our Naan, Tandoori Chicken, and Seekh Kebab so irresistible.

Our chefs hand-roll every piece of naan, fold every momo dumpling by hand, and grind fresh spice blends each morning. There are no shortcuts — only a genuine commitment to craft and flavour.
